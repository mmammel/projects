# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
The goal of this project is to create an interactive 3D polyhedron visualization and manipulation tool using WebGL. It aims to provide a fun and engaging platform for users to explore complex geometric shapes directly in their browser.

## Goals
- **Visualize 3D Polyhedra:** Enable high-performance rendering of complex 3D shapes in the browser using WebGL.
- **Interactive Manipulation:** Provide intuitive controls for users to rotate, zoom, and inspect models, including "exploding" faces to see internal structures.
- **Custom Shape Management:** Allow users to define, save, and load their own custom polyhedra definitions.
- **Fun & Exploration:** Prioritize an enjoyable user experience that encourages experimentation with geometry.

## Target Users
- **Graphics Enthusiasts:** Individuals interested in WebGL, shaders, and 3D rendering techniques.
- **Researchers & Hobbyists:** Users exploring polyhedral structures, geometry, and mathematical shapes.

## Key Features
- **Interactive 3D Viewer:** Full support for rotation, zooming, and touch/mouse interaction.
- **Geometry Tools:** Advanced manipulation features such as face explosion, edge midpoint generation, and trisection.
- **Persistence:** A system to save and retrieve custom shape definitions via an API.
- **Future Integration:** (Planned) Integration with external 3D printing services to physically realize user-designed shapes.

## Vision
To build a comprehensive and enjoyable sandbox for geometric exploration that bridges the gap between digital design and physical realization, ultimately allowing users to hold their creations in their hands.
